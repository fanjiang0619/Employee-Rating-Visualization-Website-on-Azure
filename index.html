Our Motivation
Google, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft, Facebook and Netflix are some of the big names we may think of when we are looking for jobs. It is definitely exciting when you get an offer from them. However, it is also important to check what others say about these companies. As an employee evaluation system, Glassdoor does a great job on recording what the current and former employees rate their company. In this project, we used a web scraped dataset from Glassdoor, which contains over 50k employee reviews from Google, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft, Facebook and Netflix. With our visuals, you can have a better comparison between those companies. And hope these visuals can help you make a brighter decision about where to launch you career!

Tools We Use

The tools we used to build these visuals include R Studio, JavaScript, CSS, and D3.js and Chart.js. We firstly downloaded our dataset from Kaggle. You can find our data source by clicking on "Our Dataset" button at the top of this page. Then, we used R Studio to do data manipulation and saved the prepared data in ".csv" format. After that, we started to use D3.js and Chart.js to build our visuals. Some of our visuals are partially built on the D3 visual templates imported from the official D3 GitHub Gallery.
